Interactive World Map
Leaflet.js + OpenStreetMap tiles.
Click any location or simple search (lat/long fallback).

Real-Time Data Fetch (Dynamic factors — not restricted)
Temperature, precipitation (Open-Meteo Weather API).
Air quality (AQI/PM2.5 via Open-Meteo Air Quality API).
Population density (pre-loaded lightweight WorldPop-derived JSON lookup).
Additional factors (elevation, basic proxies) if returned quickly.
Graceful fallback to cached examples for 8–10 global locations.

AI Suitability Scoring
Scores 0–100 for exactly four categories: Agriculture, Housing, Industry, Renewables.
LLM (via Vercel AI SDK + Grok/Gemini/Claude) dynamically weighs available factors.

Dashboard & Visuals
Radar/spider chart comparing the four scores.
Color-coded map pin/markers (green = high suitability).
“Why this score?” explanation cards for each category.

Generative AI Action Plan
One-click button that outputs short, practical recommendations (e.g., “High agriculture potential — consider drought-resistant crops” or “Strong solar suitability due to low population and clear conditions”).
